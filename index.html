EU AI Act · Regulation (EU) 2024/1689
AI Act Compliance Framework
Our AI systems operating in energy infrastructure, bioprocess control, and carbon markets are classified and governed under the EU AI Act's high-risk framework. Every model includes a conformity assessment, technical documentation, human oversight mechanisms, and logging of all AI-generated decisions. Prohibited practices (subliminal manipulation, social scoring) are explicitly excluded by design.
High-Risk Category Conformity Assessment Human Oversight AI Decision Logging Technical Documentation

Zero Trust · NIST SP 800-207
Zero Trust Architecture — No Implicit Trust
Every identity, device, and workload is verified continuously — never trusted implicitly regardless of network location. Our OCI Sovereign Cloud enforces Zero Trust across 6 layers: governance & identity, secure supply chain, network perimeter, data sovereignty, OKE runtime defense, and full audit observability. Every sensor is a verified identity. Every API call carries a cryptographically signed token.
NIST SP 800-207 mTLS Everywhere Continuous Verification Micro-Segmentation OCI OKE Hardened

GDPR · EU Data Sovereignty
Privacy by Design & Data Residency
All data processing architectures implement GDPR Article 25 privacy by design and by default. Sensitive operational and personal data is hosted exclusively in OCI Oslo Region (Norway) — never leaving EU/EEA jurisdiction. Customer-managed encryption keys (FIPS 140-2 Level 3 HSM) ensure no cloud provider, including Oracle, can access your data. Data minimisation enforced at every layer.

NIS2 · Critical Infrastructure
NIS2 Directive — Energy Sector Compliance
Energy platforms and EPCIC systems fall within NIS2's critical infrastructure scope. Our architecture enforces mandatory incident detection and reporting pipelines, supply chain security controls, board-level accountability structures, and multi-factor authentication across all privileged access. Cloud Guard provides continuous threat detection with automated responder recipes for sub-minute incident response.
